About Me

I work at PSRL, IIT Kanpur, as a part of the Department of Materials Science and Engineering IIT Kanpur. I work on multiphase turbulent fluid flow modeling, as part of my PhD. My main tool of analysis is OpenFOAM where I am working on the volume of fluid and lagrangian particle tracking methods, coupled to species transport and interfacial mass transfer, and reaction modeling. The work is of significance to high temperature purification of molten metals such as steel.
